# Resume Bullets — Multivariate SPC for Semiconductor Manufacturing

## Recommended Project Title (for resume)

**Multivariate Statistical Process Control for Semiconductor Manufacturing**

---

## Top 3 Resume Bullets

Paste these directly under the project title on your resume:

---

**Bullet 1 — Technical depth & dimensionality reduction**

> Engineered a multivariate SPC pipeline in Python analyzing 1,500 wafer runs across 590 correlated process variables; applied PCA to reduce dimensionality to 5 principal components capturing ≥80% of total process variance, enabling tractable monitoring of a high-dimensional semiconductor fab dataset.

---

**Bullet 2 — Anomaly detection methodology**

> Implemented Hotelling's T² and SPE/Q-residual control statistics with chi-squared UCLs (95% confidence) to detect process excursions in both the retained PC subspace and the residual space; flagged ~75 injected anomalous wafer runs from 1,500 observations with a false positive rate consistent with theoretical expectations.

---

**Bullet 3 — Engineering workflow & visualization**

> Developed a modular 5-script Python analysis pipeline (data generation, preprocessing, PCA, statistics, plotting) producing 7 engineering-grade visualizations including scree plots, T² and SPE control charts, and pairwise PC score plots to support data-driven process monitoring decisions.
